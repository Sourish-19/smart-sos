# Smart SOS

Smart SOS is an intelligent emergency alert and response application designed to provide rapid assistance in critical situations. Leveraging modern technologies, the application enables users to quickly send SOS signals, share their real-time location, and notify emergency contacts or authorities efficiently.


> **Predict. Prevent. Protect.**
>
> **Your 24×7 AI Virtual Health Guardian.**
>
> SmartSOS doesn't just track vitals — it predicts risks. From detecting gradual trends to triggering automatic SOS alerts, we ensure help arrives before an emergency happens.

---

## Features

- **One-Tap SOS Alert:** Instantly send emergency alerts with your live location.
- **Location Tracking:** Sends your real-time coordinates to predefined emergency contacts.
- **Customizable Emergency Contacts:** Manage and update your list of key contacts easily.
- **Background Service:** Keeps running to ensure help can be summoned at any time.
- **Intuitive UI:** Simple and user-friendly interface for rapid response under stress.
- **Health Monitoring (AI-Driven):** Real-time health vitals, predictions, and risk alerts using AI.
- **Guardian Dashboard:** Visual display of heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, and health trends.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- Android Studio or other compatible IDE
- Android device/emulator
- Google Play Services (for location APIs)

### Installation

1. **Clone the repository:**
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/Sourish-19/smart-sos.git
    ```
2. **Open in Android Studio** (or your preferred IDE).
3. **Build and run** the app on your device/emulator.

### Configuration

- Add your emergency contacts within the app settings or hardcode them in your local configuration as needed.
- Ensure permissions for location and SMS/notifications are granted.

## Usage

1. Launch the Smart SOS app.
2. Set up your emergency contacts in the settings menu.
3. To send an SOS alert, tap the designated SOS button.
4. Your emergency message with real-time location will be sent to your contacts for swift assistance.
5. Monitor your health stats and AI-based risk alerts on the Guardian Dashboard.
